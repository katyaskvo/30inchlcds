Apple Cinema M9179ll/A

Apple Cinema Display 30-inch is an older version designed to complement the Power Macintosh G5 and PowerBook G4 models. This wide format comes housed in an aluminum case with a sleek design.

With the optional VESA mount, you can wall-mount the display in your lobby, kiosk, or trade show booth, or in your conference room for superb video conferencing. Apple Cinema Displays feature a smooth-motion hinge that lets you adjust the tilt from -5° to +25°, that makes this model tilt and display effortlessly.

The display is built with its own I/O panel, including two FireWire 400 ports, and a self-powered, two-port USB 2.0 hub, so you can conveniently connect transient devices, or you can connect more permanent peripherals such as scanners and printers without tying up your computer's ports. There's also a security slot, so you can install a Kensington lock.

The 30" Apple Cinema Display uses DVI to connect to your computer for a smooth, stable signal. Whether you're working on your compatible Apple computer, or a PC running a video card that supports DVI with DDC technology, you’ll get crisp, sharp images in rich, lifelike color. The Apple Cinema Display is an aesthetic delight and a powerful workmate, all in one sleek package.

Update: This model has been discontinued by Apple in July 2010 however, if you are looking for this older model, it is still available for purchase through other sources online such as used versions from other sellers to refurbished models sold through Amazon.com.

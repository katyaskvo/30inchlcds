Monoprice IPS, 10734

Treat your eyes to a big, bright, and beautiful display with this 30" IPS LED Backlit LCD Monitor from Monoprice!

This 30" monitor has been upgraded with an improved 16:10 2560x1600 resolution LG display panel, which features LED backlighting and a brightness of up to 350 cd/m². The LED backlighting ensures that the display produces its full brightness and accurate colors from the moment it is powered on, so you don't have to wait for the perfect display.

This monitor offers a full range of connectivity options, including Dual Link DVI-D, VGA, HDMI, and DisplayPort. This ensures that you can connect this monitor to any Windows, Mac®, or Linux PC, as well as to modern consumer video equipment with HDMI output, such as Blu-ray players and satellite receivers. While the monitor does not include built-in speakers, it does have audio input and output jacks (3.5mm TRS) and the monitor's volume control buttons will adjust the volume level on the output jack.

The inlcuded monitor stand features the ability to be adjusted in 4 dimensions; you can pan the monitor from side to side, tilt it up or down, raise it up or down, and rotate it between landscape and portrait orientation. If you prefer to maximize the real estate on your desk, you can remove the stand and mount the monitor to a desk or wall mount. The monitor includes both 100x100 (for compatibility) and 200x100 (for stability) VESA mounting patterns, so it will fit most desk and wall mounts.

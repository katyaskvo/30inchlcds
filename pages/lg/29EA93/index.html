LG 29EA93

The LG EA93 monitor makes multi-tasking super easy with its UltraWide screen and 4-Screen Split feature. You can divide your screen into 4 customizable segments making it simple to have the information you need right in front of you. Have files on two separate devices? With Dual Link-up you can connect both devices to the monitor and view a file from each at the same time. MHL enables you to show content from compatible smartphones on this monitor without accessing the network.

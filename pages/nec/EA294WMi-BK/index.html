NEC EA294WMi-BK

The 29” NEC MultiSync EA294WMi is an LED-backlit desktop monitor ideal for corporate, financial, healthcare and broadcast environments. Designed to enhance work performance, this widescreen model features IPS panel technology with wide viewing angles (178° horizontal/vertical) and ergonomic adjustability. The LED backlighting of the EA294WMi allows for a slimmer profile, lighter weight and increased power savings. The EA294WMi features picture by picture capability, thus allowing users to essentially replace two 19" SXGA displays.

Eizo Nanao ColorEdge CG303W-BK

The ColorEdge CG303W 29.8" Widescreen LCD Computer Display from EIZO is a high-quality monitor that is built to the stringent standards required in color-critical environments.

The monitor is designed for use in environments where accurate color is essential. It features a factory calibrated gamma, a monitor hood, brightness stabilization, and ColorNavigator calibration software. It can be used in portrait or landscape orientation thanks to its pivot functionality, making it ideal for a many different design applications.

Note: This model has been discontinued by Eizo Nanao.
